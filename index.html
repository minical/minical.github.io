Open Source Booking Management Software

miniCal is an open-source booking management platform for
all businesses that requires booking/appointment scheduling.

Demo Source Code

What's in the box

Intuitive Calendar

Simple interface that provides quick overview of your property.

Reports and Analytics

Get all kinds of reports which you can also export.

CRM

Manage customer profiles along with their account balances.

Inventory

Control room availabilities of your property.

Multi-language

Customize and translate to any language all your content, including personalized emails.

Extension

Explore the extensions that empower your business.

User Management

Manage multiple user's roles and roles privilege.

Invoices

Creation and automatic delivery of the invoices to your clients by mail.
